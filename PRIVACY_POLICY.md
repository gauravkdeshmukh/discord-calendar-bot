# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 6, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how calendar-bot-by-gd ("the Bot") handles your information.

## 1. Data We Collect
When you use the `/create_event` command, the Bot temporarily processes:
- Your Discord display name (used only to label the event creator in the confirmation message)
- Event details you provide: title, date, time, duration, and description

## 2. Data We Do Not Collect
- We do not store any data in a database or on any server
- We do not collect your Discord ID, email, or any account information
- We do not track usage history or command logs beyond what your hosting environment may generate

## 3. How Your Data Is Used
Event details you provide are sent directly to Google Calendar via the Google Calendar API to create the requested event. No data is retained after the event is created.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The Bot uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:
- **Discord** — [https://discord.com/privacy](https://discord.com/privacy)
- **Google Calendar API** — [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 5. Data Retention
The Bot does not retain any user data. Google Calendar stores the event you create according to Google's own retention policies.

## 6. Children's Privacy
This Bot is not directed at children under 13. It follows Discord's minimum age requirements.

## 7. Changes
This policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance.

## 8. Contact
For questions or concerns, open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
